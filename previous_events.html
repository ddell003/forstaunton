Wednesday Advent Bible Study

Hosted by St. Paul's United Methodist Church and Christ United Methodist Church.

Christmas is a magical time but often we are searching for more than trees, lights, and gifts. All of us hope that we will can find gifts that don't break - gifts where the batteries don't die in?

What about Hope- Love- Joy- and Peace?

Could we find something in this season more meaningful than a new crockpot? Or gadget? Or another sweater?

Come join us as we explore the gifts God has for us during this seaon! background or faith status- from unsure to life-long Christian! We will be led by Pastor Sarah Locke and Pastor Bryson Smith as we consider what Christmas could be.

About us

Most of us don't hesitate to let people know what we are for- a sport's team, our favorite water hole, a political candidate, or a particular agenda. We are not shy to share.

What if we could imagine a community where everyone was For the same thing- the healing and wholeness of our beloved City?

We are For Staunton!

We believe that when we are FOR our Kids, their futures will be brighter.

We believe that when we are FOR Families, our communities will be safer and more vibrant.

We believe that when we are FOR our Senior, they receive the care they need in every stage.

We believe that when we are FOR our community, our community will grow economically, culturally to be vibrant and diverse.

We are For Staunton because we believe that God is FOR Staunton and God is for you!
